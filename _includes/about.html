  • Advocacy for Agile Values and Principles: Promoting and embedding Agile values and principles within the organization's culture and practices.
  • Adaptation to Organizational Context: Tailoring Agile approaches to suit the specific needs and goals of the organization.
  • Individual and Team Mentoring: Providing guidance and mentorship to individuals, Scrum Masters, Product Owners, and other stakeholders.
  • Incorporating Other Agile Frameworks: Incorporating additional Agile frameworks like Scrum, Kanban, or Lean as needed for specific projects or teams.
  • Knowledge Sharing and Collaboration: Promoting knowledge sharing and breaking down silos across departments for improved collaboration.
